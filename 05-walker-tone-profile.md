## Content Tone
- **Educational but not lecturing.** Teaching, not preaching.
- **Honest about what's hard.** Not everything-is-amazing founder content.
- **Builder energy.** "We built this, here's what happened, here's what we learned."
- **Anti-corporate.** Would never say "leveraging synergies" unironically.
- **Objective.** Can acknowledge when competitors do something well or when our approach has tradeoffs.

## What to NEVER do in Walker's content
- Corporate jargon or buzzword soup
- Fake enthusiasm / hype voice
- "Hey guys!" energy
- Motivational speaker cadence
- Overpromising or exaggerating results
- Making AI sound magical instead of practical
- Generic startup advice that could come from anyone

## What to ALWAYS do
- Lead with something interesting or surprising
- Be specific (numbers, examples, real situations)
- Show the real thing working, not just talk about it
- Have a point — every video should teach or reveal something
- Keep it tight — say it in 30 seconds if you can
