# YouTube Guide (Long-Form + Shorts)

## Long-Form Specs
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum, 4K preferred
- Length sweet spot: 8-15 minutes
- Thumbnail: 1280x720, high contrast, face + text
- Title: under 60 characters, keyword-rich

## YouTube = TEACH, Not Just Entertain
Short-form is entertainment first. YouTube long-form is where we build trust and authority.

## Content Pillars
| Pillar | % | Description |
|--------|---|-------------|
| Building in Public | 30% | Real startup journey, decisions, wins/fails |
| AI Education | 25% | Teach something valuable about AI |
| Business Strategy | 20% | Lessons on strategy, fundraising, PMF |
| Tech Deep Dives | 15% | Architecture, code walkthroughs |
| Industry Analysis | 10% | Market trends, competitor breakdowns |

## Video Structure Templates

### "How We Built X"
0:00 HOOK: Show end result first (30s)
0:30 THE PROBLEM: What we were solving
2:00 THE APPROACH: Our thinking, options considered
4:00 THE BUILD: Walk through implementation
8:00 THE RESULT: Demo, before/after, impact
10:00 LESSONS: What we'd do differently
11:00 CTA: Subscribe, next video tease

### "What is [Concept]"
0:00 HOOK: Why this matters (30s)
0:30 THE OLD WAY: How things work now
2:00 THE NEW WAY: Introduce concept clearly
4:00 HOW IT WORKS: Step by step, visual
7:00 REAL EXAMPLES: Show in action
9:00 WHY IT MATTERS: Bigger picture
10:00 CTA

### "Startup Lesson"
0:00 HOOK: Lesson in one sentence (15s)
0:15 THE STORY: What happened
2:00 THE CONTEXT: Why it mattered
3:00 THE DECISION: What we did
5:00 THE OUTCOME: What happened
7:00 THE FRAMEWORK: Generalize for viewer
9:00 APPLICATION: How viewer applies this
10:00 CTA

### "Industry Breakdown"
0:00 HOOK: Surprising stat or bold claim (30s)
0:30 LANDSCAPE: Where industry is now
3:00 KEY PLAYERS: Who's doing what
5:00 THE GAP: What nobody is solving
7:00 OUR TAKE: What we think happens next
9:00 IMPLICATIONS: What it means for audience
10:00 CTA

## Thumbnail Principles
1. Face with emotion (curiosity, shock)
2. Bold text: 3-5 words max, readable on mobile
3. High contrast
4. Curiosity gap: thumbnail + title = NEED to click
5. Consistent branding across videos

## Shorts Strategy
Sources: Clips from long-form, TikTok repurpose (no watermark), standalone quick hits.
"Liked this? Full breakdown on the channel." drives to long-form.

## YouTube > Short-Form Pipeline
Every long-form video should produce 3-5 Shorts:
1. Best 30-second clip
2. Hook/cold open as standalone
3. "The craziest part is..." moment
4. Hot take or contrarian statement
5. Biggest reveal/punchline
