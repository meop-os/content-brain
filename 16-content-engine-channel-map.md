|---------|---------|----------|------|-----------|

## Funnel Logic
SHORT-FORM + FACEBOOK (broad): "What's possible. Here's the vision."
> Builds massive following. No selling.
LINKEDIN + X (targeted): "Here's how this works for YOUR business."
> Educates business owners. Builds trust.
YOUTUBE LONG-FORM (deep): "Let me show you exactly what this looks like."
> Deep dives, case studies, proof.
EMAIL + NEWSLETTER (conversion): "You've seen it. Here's how to start."
> Direct offers. Close the deal.

## Caption Rewrite by Platform

### TikTok / Reels / Shorts
- 1-3 lines max
- Hook in first line
- 3-5 hashtags
- End with question/challenge
- Tone: casual, punchy

### Facebook
- 3-5 lines
- Conversational, shareable
- Include a question
- No hashtags (or 1-2 max)

### LinkedIn
- 5-15 lines (one per thought, skip lines)
- Hook in first line
- Business-focused
- CTA at end
- 3-5 hashtags at bottom

### X (Twitter)
- 1-2 sentences max
- Sharp, quotable
- No hashtags unless trending

### YouTube Shorts
- Title: 5-8 words, curiosity-driven
- Description: 2-3 sentences with keywords
- Tags: 5-8 relevant terms

## Static Post Types
1. Quote Cards: Walker's one-liner, dark background, white text
2. Insight Graphics: One stat, visually formatted
3. Before/After: "Without AI" vs "With AI"
4. Carousel Posts: 5-8 slides, one idea per slide
5. Thread Graphics: Quote card versions of a thread
6. Data Cards: One big number + context line

## Static Post Design Specs
- Background: Dark (#07080d) or clean white
- Text: White on dark
- Accent: MEOP blue or green
- Font: Inter Bold (headlines), Inter Regular (body)
- Dimensions: 1080x1080 (feed), 1080x1350 (portrait), 1080x1920 (story)
- Handle: @walkerbauknight on every card
