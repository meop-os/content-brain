# CTA Playbook
Rule: No content goes live without a call to action.

## CTA Types

### 1. ENGAGEMENT (Build Audience)
- Question: "What would you automate first?"
- Tag someone: "Tag your cofounder who needs this"
- Debate: "Agree or disagree?"
- Share: "Send this to someone building something"

### 2. GROWTH (Build Following)
- "Follow for more startup content"
- "Part 2 drops tomorrow. Follow so you don't miss it."
- "Subscribe. New video every week."

### 3. TRAFFIC (Drive to Destination)
- "Link in bio for the full breakdown"
- "DM me 'AVAI' and I'll send you the deck"
- "Comment 'INFO' and I'll send it to you"

### 4. CONVERSION (Business Outcomes)
- "Book a demo at meop.cloud"
- "Join the waitlist. Link in bio."
- "DM me if you want to see it in action"

## CTA by Platform
- TikTok: Engagement first. Never break entertainment to sell.
- IG Reels: "Save this for later" is the most powerful IG CTA.
- IG Stories: Every story needs an interactive element.
- YouTube: "If you learned something, subscribe." Only after delivering value.
- LinkedIn: Open-ended question > link. Algorithm rewards comments over clicks.
- X/Twitter: Sharp, quotable standalone thought.

## Weekly CTA Rotation
- Mon: Engagement (question/debate)
- Tue: Growth (follow/subscribe)
- Wed: Traffic (link/DM keyword)
- Thu: Engagement (tag/share)
- Fri: Conversion (demo/waitlist)
- Sat: Engagement (fun/casual)
- Sun: Growth (series hook)

## Anti-Patterns
1. "Link in bio" on every post (gets ignored)
2. Hard sell on TikTok (instant scroll)
3. CTA that doesn't match content
4. No CTA at all (wasted content)
5. Multiple CTAs in one piece (pick ONE)
6. Asking for follows in first 3 seconds (earn it first)
