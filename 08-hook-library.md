# Hook Library

## Hook Types That Work

### 1. Curiosity Gap
- "I built an AI that [unexpected thing]. Here's what happened."
- "There are 50 companies doing X. Zero doing Y."
- "An engineering lead at a top firm said something I can't stop thinking about."

### 2. Contrarian Statement
- "Corporate branding is dead."
- "Stop building AI products. Build AI employees."
- "The busiest person in your office is doing the least important work."

### 3. Number/Data Hook
- "I counted the tools it takes to schedule one meeting. Seven."
- "40% of my time was on work that didn't need me."
- "$125K per partner per year. Lost to admin work."

### 4. Story Open
- "I wanted two computers to share a brain. So I built one."
- "Two months ago I was building a business education platform. Then I pivoted."
- "Had a call with someone who leads engineering at one of the top firms in the world."

### 5. Challenge/Question
- "What would you do with 10 extra hours a week?"
- "It's 2026 and you're still manually reviewing sales calls."
- "Name one good reason your team does CRM updates by hand."

### 6. Pattern Interrupt (short-form)
- "POV: You're a college student CTO and your AI just..."
- Start mid-sentence: "...and that's when I realized nobody had built this."
- Visual hook: screen recording of something unexpected

## Fill-in-the-Blank Hook Formulas

1. "Everyone's building [X]. We're building [Y]."
2. "I counted [number] [things]. It's insane."
3. "[Authority figure] just said [bold claim]. He's right."
4. "Stop [common practice]. Start [better thing]."
5. "It's 2026 and you're still [outdated practice]."
6. "Nobody's talking about [hidden trend]."
7. "There are [X] companies doing [A]. Zero doing [B]."
8. "I showed [impressive thing] to [credible person]. Their reaction:"
9. "[Controversial claim]. Here's the math."
10. "I built [thing] in [short time]. It [impressive result]."
11. "The [industry] has had [years] to fix this. They didn't."
12. "[Simple question] turned into a [big realization]."

## Platform-Specific Hook Rules
| Platform | Hook Window | Format |
|----------|------------|--------|
| TikTok | First 1-2 seconds | Visual or spoken |
| Instagram Reels | First 2 seconds | Same as TikTok |
| YouTube Shorts | First 3 seconds | Slightly more room |
| LinkedIn | First line (before "see more") | Text hook |
| X/Twitter | First sentence | One punchy line |
| YouTube Long | First 15 seconds | Hook + promise |
