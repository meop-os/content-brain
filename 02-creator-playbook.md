## WHAT TO STEAL FROM EACH

### Justin Welsh (162M+ impressions)
- **The Trailer system**: Hook (3 short lines) → Meat → TL;DR → Call-to-Conversation
- **Content Matrix**: 1 topic × 7 angles = 7 posts from 1 idea (teaching, observation, prediction, comparison, teardown, listicle, contrarian)
- **Batching**: 20 posts in 1 hour using templates
- **Link trick**: Post WITHOUT links first, edit to add links after algorithm scores it
- **Text-only dominance**: Most posts are pure text. Formatting IS the visual.
- **Anti-list hooks**: "No ads. No selfies. No videos. No employees." — define by rejection

### Matt Lakajev (95K+ followers, 11.24% engagement)
- **One-line-per-thought**: Blank line between every sentence. Mobile-first.
- **Specific numbers always**: Never "a lot of money" — always "$247,707"
- **Comment CTAs for lead capture**: "Comment 'X' and I'll send it free"
- **Story + Authority mix**: Personal transformation stories alternate with system reveals
- **4A framework**: Actionable, Analytical, Aspirational, Anthropological

### Ruben Hassid (793K followers, 120M+ impressions)
- **16+ posts/week**: Volume is the strategy. 2/day minimum.
- **Image posts dominate (54%)**: AI-generated graphics with text overlays, NOT photos
- **Hook max 55 characters per line**: Must fit before "...see more"
- **Numbered steps as default body format**: If it can be a list, it is a list
- **Repost CTA**: Always ends with "Repost this to help someone [verb] today"
- **Dark background + orange accent**: Signature visual brand on all graphics
- **Utility over personality**: 80% actionable how-to, 20% opinion/personal
- **BREAKING: news format**: "[Company] just [did thing]. Here's what it means."

## WALKER'S CONTENT SYSTEM

### Voice Rules (from brand-voice.ts — NON-NEGOTIABLE)
- Starts sentences lowercase. Don't fight it.
- Short punchy lines. No filler. Cut every word you can.
- Specific over generic. Always.
- Never sounds like he's trying to impress anyone.
- No em dashes. Ever.
- Never start with "I."
- One idea per post.
- No: "Here's the thing" / "Let me be honest" / "Most people don't realize" / "Game-changer" / "Excited to announce"

### Post Types × Formats

| Type | Format | Walker's face? | Image? | Frequency |
|------|--------|---------------|--------|-----------|
| **News take** | BREAKING: + 3-5 line analysis | No | AI graphic with headline | Daily |
| **Educational** | Numbered steps / framework | Yes — face composite | Face + text overlay | 3-4x/week |
| **Hot take** | Bold claim + no hedging | Optional | Text-only or minimal graphic | 2-3x/week |
| **Founder story** | Personal moment + lesson | Yes | Face photo or text-only | 1-2x/week |
| **Tool/demo** | Screen recording + insight | Yes (if teaching) | Video or screenshot | 1-2x/week |

### Hook Templates (adapted for Walker's topics)

| Pattern | Template | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **BREAKING news** | "[Company] just [action]. [One-line implication]." | "Google just open-sourced their browser fingerprinting dataset. Every ad network is panicking." |
| **Specific number** | "[Number] [things] in [timeframe]. [Unexpected detail]." | "47 data points leaked in 1.9 seconds. No clicks. No permissions." |
| **Contrarian** | "Everyone says [common wisdom]. [Why it's wrong]." | "Everyone says VPNs protect you. Your browser gave away your GPU model before the VPN connected." |
| **Anti-list** | "No [thing]. No [thing]. No [thing]. Just [what matters]." | "No popups. No permissions. No consent forms. Just silent data collection." |
| **Direct accusation** | "Your [thing] is [uncomfortable truth]." | "Your browser is a surveillance device you carry voluntarily." |
| **Comparison** | "[Known thing] was scary. [New thing] is worse." | "Phishing was scary. Passive browser fingerprinting is worse. And it's legal." |

### Body Structure (every post)

```
HOOK (1-2 lines, under 55 chars/line)
[blank line]
CONTEXT (1-2 sentences, why this matters)
[blank line]
MEAT (numbered steps, framework, or insight — 3-8 lines)
[blank line]
CLOSER (the point — not a question, not a CTA, unless lead capture)
```

For LinkedIn specifically:
- One line per thought
- Blank line between every line
- 6-12 lines total
- No hashtags. No emojis.
