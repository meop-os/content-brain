## Reference 1: Instagram Carousel (2026-02-12)
**Post:** "When you die, your life will be summarized into facts about your early life..."
**Engagement:** 16.9K likes in 4 days

### Design Breakdown

**Layout:**
- Carousel format (6+ slides)
- Slide 1: Text-heavy quote card + lifestyle photo
- Clean split — text on left, image on right
- Photo is candid, authentic (Hormozi + Leila laughing)

**Typography:**
- Bold, high-contrast white/dark text
- Large font — readable at phone scroll speed
- Serif or bold sans-serif for quotes
- Name/handle prominently displayed with verified badge
- Quote attribution style: profile pic + @handle + blue check

**Color Palette:**
- Muted/warm photo tones
- Text overlay areas: semi-transparent or solid color blocks
- High contrast for readability
- NOT flashy or neon — sophisticated, clean

**Content Pattern:**
- **The Hook:** Perspective-shifting statement ("When you die...")
- **The Build:** Paints the picture (1-3 anecdotes, qualities, survived by)
- **The Flip:** Makes you reconsider ("Whatever you're stressed about won't make the list")
- **The Extension:** First comment adds depth (10 years ago vs 10 years from now)

### What Makes It Work
1. **Universal truth** — everyone can relate
2. **Zoom-out perspective** — makes your problems feel small
3. **NOT selling anything** — pure value builds trust
4. **Candid photo** — not posed, feels real
5. **First comment strategy** — extends the thought, boosts algorithm
6. **Engagement is emoji-heavy** — proves emotional resonance

## Hormozi Static Content Patterns (General)

### Quote Cards
- Clean background (dark or white)
- One powerful statement per card
- Large, bold text
- Minimal design — no clutter
- Hormozi's face/brand at bottom

### Carousel Posts
- 5-10 slides
- Slide 1: Hook (must work as standalone image in feed)
- Middle slides: One idea per slide, build the argument
- Last slide: CTA or perspective shift
- Consistent design across all slides

### Data/Stat Graphics
- One big number, bold
- Context line below
- Clean, minimal design
- Usually dark background

### Design Rules to Steal
1. **Readability > aesthetics** — if you can't read it scrolling, it fails
2. **One idea per slide** — never cram
3. **Authentic photos > stock** — real moments, candid shots
4. **Text that works WITHOUT the image** — screenshot-shareable
5. **Consistent brand look** — recognize it's Hormozi before reading the name
