# CTA Playbook — Every Piece of Content Gets One
**Created:** 2026-02-10
**Rule:** No content goes live without a call to action. Period.

---

## CTA Philosophy

Every piece of content exists to do TWO things:
1. **Deliver value** (entertain, educate, inspire)
2. **Move the viewer somewhere** (follow, click, comment, share, buy)

The CTA doesn't have to be aggressive. It has to exist.

---

## CTA Types by Intent

### 1. ENGAGEMENT CTAs (Build Audience)
**Goal:** Get interaction. Boost algorithm. Build community.

| CTA | Best For | Example |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Question | Short-form, LinkedIn | "What would you automate first?" |
| Poll/This-or-that | IG Stories, TikTok | "Which one? A or B?" |
| Tag someone | IG, TikTok | "Tag your cofounder who needs to see this" |
| Debate prompt | LinkedIn, Twitter/X | "Agree or disagree?" |
| Share prompt | All | "Send this to someone building something" |
| Duet/Stitch | TikTok | "Stitch this with your version" |

### 2. GROWTH CTAs (Build Following)
**Goal:** Convert viewer to follower.

| CTA | Best For | Example |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Follow for more | TikTok, IG Reels | "Follow for more startup content" |
| Series hook | All | "Part 2 drops tomorrow. Follow so you don't miss it." |
| Subscribe | YouTube | "Subscribe — new video every week" |
| Turn on notifications | YouTube, IG | "Hit the bell" |
| Connect | LinkedIn | "Connect with me — I post about [topic] daily" |

### 3. TRAFFIC CTAs (Drive to Destination)
**Goal:** Move people off-platform to our properties.

| CTA | Best For | Example |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Link in bio | IG, TikTok | "Link in bio for the full breakdown" |
| Website | LinkedIn, YouTube | "meop.cloud for more" |
| DM me | IG, LinkedIn | "DM me 'AVAI' and I'll send you the deck" |
| Comment keyword | TikTok, IG | "Comment 'INFO' and I'll send it to you" |
| YouTube end screen | YouTube | Card + end screen to next video |

### 4. CONVERSION CTAs (Business Outcomes)
**Goal:** Turn attention into revenue, partnerships, or investment.

| CTA | Best For | Example |
|-----|----------|---------|
| Book a demo | LinkedIn, YouTube, Email | "Book a demo at meop.cloud" |
| Join waitlist | All | "Join the waitlist — link in bio" |
| Schedule a call | LinkedIn | "DM me if you want to see it in action" |
| Download resource | LinkedIn, YouTube | "Free guide in the description" |
| Apply/Hire | LinkedIn | "We're hiring — link in bio" |

## CTA by Platform

### TikTok
- **Primary:** Engagement (comment, share, follow)
- **Secondary:** Traffic (link in bio, comment keyword)
- **Tone:** Casual, never salesy. "Follow for more" works. "Check out our website" doesn't.
- **Placement:** Last 2-3 seconds of video OR in caption
- **Rule:** Never break the entertainment to sell. CTA rides the energy.

### Instagram Reels
- **Primary:** Engagement (save, share, comment)
- **Secondary:** Growth (follow, profile visit)
- **Tone:** Slightly more polished than TikTok. Still human.
- **Placement:** Caption + verbal in video
- **Rule:** "Save this for later" is the most powerful IG CTA.

### Instagram Stories
- **Primary:** Engagement (poll, question, slider)
- **Secondary:** Traffic (link sticker, DM)
- **Tone:** Raw, unfiltered, conversational
- **Placement:** Interactive stickers
- **Rule:** Every story should have an interactive element.

### YouTube (Long-form)
- **Primary:** Growth (subscribe, bell, next video)
- **Secondary:** Traffic (description links, end screen)
- **Tone:** Direct, earned. Ask after delivering value.
- **Placement:** 30-second mark (early CTA), end of video, description
- **Rule:** "If you learned something, subscribe" — only after you taught something.

### YouTube Shorts
- **Primary:** Engagement (comment, like)
- **Secondary:** Growth (subscribe, full video link)
- **Tone:** Fast, punchy, same energy as TikTok
- **Placement:** Last second, on-screen text
- **Rule:** Point to full YouTube video when applicable.

### LinkedIn
- **Primary:** Engagement (comment, repost)
- **Secondary:** Conversion (DM, book demo, website)
- **Tone:** Professional but real. Never LinkedIn-bro energy.
- **Placement:** Last 2-3 lines of post
- **Rule:** Open-ended question > link. Algorithm rewards comments over clicks.
- **Format:** Walker's voice rules apply (see `campaigns/AVAI_POST_META_PROMPT.md`)

## CTA Rotation Strategy

Don't use the same CTA every time. Rotate:

**Week pattern:**
- Mon: Engagement (question/debate)
- Tue: Growth (follow/subscribe hook)
- Wed: Traffic (link in bio / DM keyword)
- Thu: Engagement (tag/share)
- Fri: Conversion (demo/waitlist/website)
- Sat: Engagement (fun/casual)
- Sun: Growth (series hook / recap)

---

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do This)

1. **"Link in bio"** on every single post — gets ignored fast
2. **Hard sell on TikTok** — instant scroll. Entertainment first, always.
3. **CTA that doesn't match content** — teaching video → "buy now" = trust broken
4. **No CTA at all** — wasted content. Every view is an opportunity.
5. **Multiple CTAs in one piece** — pick ONE. Clarity wins.
6. **"Check out my website"** without context — give them a reason
7. **Asking for follows in the first 3 seconds** — earn it first
