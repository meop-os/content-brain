# Scene Engine: Photography and Video Location Guide

## Concept
Treat every real-world location like a video game level. Scout it, map it, compose scenes, then shoot.

SCOUT > MAP > COMPOSE > SHOOT > GRADE > PUBLISH

## Lighting States
| Time | Lighting | Mood | Best For |
|------|----------|------|----------|
| Dawn (5:30-6:30) | Soft pink/orange | Peaceful, cinematic | Hero shots, reflective |
| Golden hour AM (6:30-7:30) | Warm gold, long shadows | Warm, inviting | Portrait, lifestyle |
| Midday (11-2) | Harsh overhead | Energetic, raw | Urban, BTS, candid |
| Shade (any time) | Soft, even | Clean, professional | Product shots, headshots |
| Golden hour PM (5:30-7:00) | Warm gold, dramatic | Premium, cinematic | Hero shots, brand |
| Blue hour (7:00-7:30) | Cool blue, city lights | Moody, tech-forward | Night grind |
| Night | Screen glow, desk lamp | Intimate, dedicated | Late-night coding |

## Phone Camera Tips
- Clean lens first, every time
- Tap to focus + expose
- Use 3x3 grid lines
- HDR on for landscapes
- Shoot highest quality
- Include foreground depth (flat landscapes are boring)
- Leading lines (roads, railings, water edges)
- Frame within frames (doorways, windows, arches)
- Shoot same spot at different times = 3 pieces from 1 location
