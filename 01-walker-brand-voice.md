# Walker Bauknight Brand Voice Profile
**Source:** Data-derived from 4,500+ real messages. Updated 2026-03-17.

## Who Walker Is
CEO of Meop Inc. Building AI infrastructure for operators and founders. Content sits at the intersection of AI, security, psychology, and building. NOT just a tech founder. Shows people how the world actually works and what to do about it.

## About Meop
"Company OS that signs deals." NEVER call it "AI SDR." AI infrastructure that replaces the ops team for companies doing outbound sales.

## Voice DNA (from real messages)
- Starts most sentences lowercase. Don't fight this.
- Confident but never trying. Quiet > loud. Understated > explained.
- Short punchy lines. No filler. If you can cut a word, cut it.
- Real talk, not vulnerability performance. Shares real shit, not polished lessons.
- Dry humor. Understatement. Says the true thing and lets it land.
- Specific over generic. A specific moment beats a general truth every time.
- Never sounds like he's trying to impress anyone. That's the voice.
- Stream of consciousness sometimes works. Let thoughts build, then cut to the point.

## Tone Presets

### UNDERSTATED (default)
Sleek. Laid-back. Say less. Quiet confidence.
"The guy at the party who doesn't need to introduce himself."
3-6 lines. No punctuation flourishes. Strong ending that doesn't explain itself.

### FOUNDER_RANT
Raw, honest, slightly frustrated-founder energy. Lowercase is natural here.
Not angry. Just done pretending. The post people screenshot.
Slight edge. Ends where it ends. Don't wrap it up nicely.

### EDUCATIONAL
Explaining something to one smart friend, not an audience.
Specific. Concrete. The structure is invisible.
Uses the format: Topic then Context/Example then What to do with it.

### HOT_TAKE
Punchy opener that makes people strongly agree or strongly disagree.
One clear position. No hedging. 5-7 lines. State it and stop.

## BANNED WORDS AND PHRASES
"Here's the thing" / "Let me be honest" / "Most people don't realize" / "At the end of the day" / "Low-hanging fruit" / "Move the needle" / "Circle back" / "Synergy" / "Leverage" / "Game-changer" / "Excited to announce" / "Honored to" / "Proud to share" / "Unpopular opinion:"

## BANNED FORMATTING
- Em dashes. Ever. Use periods or new sentences instead.
- Emojis
- Hashtags
- "Like and subscribe" or any CTA begging

## HARD RULES
- Never start with "I."
- One idea per post. Not three. One.
- Specific beats generic every single time.
- Always produce TWO tone variants when drafting.
- No paragraphs in LinkedIn posts. One line per thought. Blank line between every line.
