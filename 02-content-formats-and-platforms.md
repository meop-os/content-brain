# Content Formats and Platform Rules

## Universal Post Format (all content)
1. Hook / Topic (first 2 lines, before "see more")
2. Context / Example (specific name, number, moment)
3. Application / What to do with it

## Content Pillars (Weekly Schedule)
- Monday: Mindset. Mental models, how founders think, why most people stay stuck.
- Tuesday: Leadership. Building teams, hard conversations, hiring, delegation.
- Wednesday: Tech / Building. What's being built with AI, made accessible, no jargon.
- Thursday: Hot Take. Contrarian opinions on AI, security, business. Bold. Polarizing.
- Friday: Personal. Real moments, failures, what he learned. Not polished stories.

## LinkedIn Rules
- One line per thought. Blank line between every line.
- 5-10 words per line target.
- Hook in first 2 lines (before "see more" cutoff).
- No paragraphs. No hashtags. No emojis.
- End with the point. Not a question, not a CTA.
- 6-12 lines total.
- Post at 7:45-8:00 AM EST (breakfast scroll, commute).

## Twitter / X Rules
- 1-3 lines max for standalone tweets.
- Threads: 5-8 tweets, each one standalone-strong.
- No hashtags.

## Short Form Video Script Rules
- Hook in first 3 seconds. The opening line must stop the scroll.
- 60-90 seconds total speaking time.
- Conversational. Talking to one person, not an audience.
- End with a strong closer. No "like and subscribe."

## Video On-Camera Style
- Low baseline energy. Occasional spike. NOT a YouTuber.
- Think: Jim Halpert talking head. Deadpan. Self-aware. Dry.
- Start talking immediately. First word is the hook. No "hey guys."
- One thought per beat. Say one thing. Pause. Say the next thing.
- Let silence work. 1-2 second pauses after strong lines.
- If you mess up, keep going. Real is the brand.

## LinkedIn Funnel Structure
LinkedIn (7 posts/week) > Lead Magnet (gated download) > Email List (weekly nurture) > Warm Audience (DM conversations) > Customer

Weekly mix: 3 Lead Magnet posts + 3 Value posts + 1 Offer post

## Carousel / Image Posts
- Use Canva or Gamma for creation
- 5-8 slides for carousels
- Clean, dark/neutral tones, minimal design
- Brand colors: bg #0a0a0f, primary #00ff41, secondary #00d4ff, text #e6edf3
