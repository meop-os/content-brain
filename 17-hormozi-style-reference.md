# Hormozi Static Content Style Guide (Reference for MEOP)

## Design Patterns to Steal

### Quote Cards
- Clean background (dark or white)
- One powerful statement per card
- Large, bold text
- Minimal design, no clutter
- Name/handle at bottom

### Carousel Posts
- 5-10 slides
- Slide 1: Hook (must work as standalone image in feed)
- Middle slides: One idea per slide, build the argument
- Last slide: CTA or perspective shift
- Consistent design across all slides

### Data/Stat Graphics
- One big number, bold
- Context line below
- Clean, minimal, dark background

## Design Rules
1. Readability > aesthetics. If you can't read it scrolling, it fails.
2. One idea per slide. Never cram.
3. Authentic photos > stock. Real moments, candid shots.
4. Text that works WITHOUT the image. Screenshot-shareable.
5. Consistent brand look. Recognizable before reading the name.

## Content Patterns That Get 16K+ Likes
- The Hook: Perspective-shifting statement
- The Build: 1-3 anecdotes or qualities
- The Flip: Makes you reconsider
- The Extension: First comment adds depth (boosts algorithm)

## What Works
- Universal truth everyone relates to
- Zoom-out perspective (makes your problems feel small)
- NOT selling anything (pure value builds trust)
- Candid photo (not posed)
- First comment strategy (extends the thought)
