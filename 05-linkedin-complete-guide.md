# LinkedIn Complete Guide

## Platform Specs
- Post length: up to 3,000 characters (first 140 visible before "see more")
- Image: 1200x1200 (square) or 1200x627 (landscape)
- Carousel/PDF: up to 300 pages, 1080x1080 or 1080x1350 per slide
- Video: up to 10 min
- Best time: Tue-Thu, 7-8am or 12pm EST
- Frequency: 5x/week minimum for growth
- Algorithm priority: Comments > Shares > Reactions > Clicks

## LinkedIn vs Other Platforms
- Tone: Confident, insightful, real (not funny/chaotic like TikTok)
- Goal: Credibility, relationships, deals
- Audience: Investors, executives, operators, founders
- Content: Value-first (insight, lesson, framework)
- Success metric: Meaningful connections, DMs, opportunities

## Content Types

### 1. Insight Post (2x/week)
One clear insight about AI, startups, or the industry.
Format: Bold opening > 2-3 lines context > "so what" > CTA question

### 2. Lesson Post (1x/week)
Something learned building MEOP. Real, specific, useful.
Format: What happened > Context > What we learned > How you can apply > CTA

### 3. Category Post (1x/week)
Establishing thought leadership. Bold statement about market.
Format: Bold statement > Why now > Our perspective > Debate-worthy question

### 4. Build-in-Public Post (1x/week)
Real updates. Transparent.
Format: Milestone/update > What we did (specific) > What's next > CTA

### 5. Carousel / Document Post (1x/week)
Highest save and share rate on LinkedIn.
Slide structure:
- Slide 1: HOOK (bold claim or question)
- Slide 2-7: CONTENT (one point per slide)
- Slide 8: SUMMARY
- Slide 9: CTA (follow + comment)
- Slide 10: ABOUT (one-liner about MEOP + handle)

## CTA Best Practices
1. Open-ended question (drives comments)
2. "Repost if you agree" (extends reach)
3. DM prompt: "DM me [keyword]" (direct leads)
4. Tag prompt: "Tag someone who needs this"
5. Link in FIRST COMMENT, not post body (algorithm doesn't penalize comments with links)

## LinkedIn Funnel
LinkedIn (7 posts/week) > Lead Magnet (gated download) > Email List (weekly nurture) > Warm Audience (DMs) > Customer

Weekly mix: 3 Lead Magnet posts (Mon/Wed/Fri) + 3 Value posts (Tue/Thu/Sat) + 1 Offer post (Sun)

## Lead Magnet Post Formula
Line 1: HOOK (specific number or bold claim)
Lines 2-4: PROBLEM (describe the pain)
Lines 5-7: TEASE (what the lead magnet covers)
Lines 8-9: PROOF (one stat, one result)
Line 10: CTA ("Comment [WORD] and I'll send it to you.")

## Value Post Formula
Line 1: HOOK (contrarian statement or unexpected opener)
Lines 2-6: STORY/SETUP (build tension, short lines)
Lines 7-10: INSIGHT (the actual lesson)
Lines 11-12: REFRAME (flip the reader's thinking)
Line 13: OPEN QUESTION (drives comments)
